
            Financial Planning Basics
            
            1. Emergency Fund: Build a fund with 3-6 months of expenses before investing.
            2. Debt Management: Pay off high-interest debts before major investments.
            3. Retirement Planning: Contribute to retirement accounts regularly.
            4. Housing: Keep housing costs below 30% of income.
            5. Investments: Diversify your portfolio across asset classes.
            6. Insurance: Ensure adequate life, health, and property insurance.
            7. Tax Planning: Optimize tax strategies for savings and investments.
